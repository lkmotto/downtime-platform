Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

DownTime ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

DownTime collects minimal data to provide event discovery:

  • Email address — only if you subscribe to the weekly digest
  • General location — city/region level only, to find events near you. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification documents
  • We do not collect financial or payment information
  • We do not collect contacts, call logs, or SMS data
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

How We Use Your Information

Your email (if provided) is used solely to send the weekly event digest. Your general location is used to find relevant events in your area. That's it.

Data Storage

Email addresses are stored securely and can be deleted at any time by contacting us. Event data is sourced from public event platforms and is not stored permanently.

Third-Party Services

We use Resend for email delivery. Their privacy policy is available at resend.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Children's Privacy

DownTime is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
